Frequently Asked

FAQ.

Honest answers to the questions we get most often.

Once SEBI grants our broker license, buy and sell functionality goes live. Until then, Grath is view-only — you can browse charts and indices, but cannot place orders. We do not give a date because we cannot control the regulatory timeline. We will email every registered user the day it changes.
Today, no money sits with us — we are pre-launch. When SARKSH Trade goes live, your shares sit in your demat in your name. SARKSH never holds custody of customer assets. This is enforced by SEBI regulation, and we will not deviate from it.
Because building our own market data infrastructure before we have a broker license would be wasteful. TradingView is a well-known global charting service, used by Zerodha, Upstox, and most Indian brokers. The charts you see are accurate and live. When SARKSH Trade launches, this becomes our own native data layer.
Raise a ticket from the Support tab with category "Bug or technical glitch". Include what you were doing, what you expected, what actually happened. The faster you tell us, the faster we fix it. Bug reports during the test phase are genuinely valued — we will personally thank you.
Email sarkshgrowmore@gmail.com from the email address you registered with. Subject: "Account deletion". We confirm within 24 hours and remove your account from all systems within 7 days, retaining only what we are legally required to retain.
Profile editing requires KYC verification, which goes live with SARKSH Trade. Until then, write to support if either changes — we will manually update your record after verifying your identity.

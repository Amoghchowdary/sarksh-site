SARKSH GROW privacy policy for website forms, webinar bookings, analytics and customer communication.

Privacy overview

SARKSH GROW collects visitor details only when a visitor submits a form, books a webinar, contacts support or uses a linked communication channel.

Information collected through forms

  • Name, phone number and email address.
  • Preferred webinar topic or product interest.
  • Message, city and scheduling preference when provided.
  • Page source and submission time for operational tracking.

How it is used

Information is used to respond to enquiries, schedule webinars, manage support requests, improve communication and maintain basic operational records.

Analytics

The website may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Microsoft Clarity after configuration. These tools help understand page visits, clicks, scroll behaviour and user experience. Sensitive form inputs should not be publicly displayed.

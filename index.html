FAQ

Questions people should ask. Before they act.

Is SARKSH GROW a broker?

SARKSH GROW is presented as a finance and technology company focused on market education, product pathways and customer support. Public content does not claim broker status or make regulated-service claims beyond what is clearly stated.

Can SARKSH tell me which stock to buy?

No public website content or free webinar should be treated as personalised advice or a stock recommendation.

Why mention biggest broker in India and India’s number one broker?

Because customers search those phrases. SARKSH uses them as educational topics to explain that size, popularity and advertising are not the only factors when choosing a broker or trading platform.

Where do webinar bookings go?

Bookings are submitted to the existing Google Apps Script backend connected to the website. The payload includes name, phone, email, preferred topic, preferred timing and message.

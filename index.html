01 · About Us

A finance company built for people entering the market.

The customer should understand the market language before hearing a product pitch. That is the operating culture of SARKSH GROW.

2025

Today, SARKSH GROW provides free market awareness and education. The company is preparing product pathways in trading, portfolio management, and expertise-led advisory intelligence. Regulated services will move forward only after applicable approvals, licensing readiness, and compliance controls.

04 · Learning

A visitor should leave with more knowledge than they came with.

These learning topics are placed on the website because the public must understand finance before choosing a platform, broker, product or advisor.

How investing works

Investing begins with ownership, time horizon, risk capacity and discipline. A beginner should first understand how money enters the market, where securities are held, and how exits are handled.

Demat and trading accounts

A demat account holds securities. A trading account places orders. A bank account moves funds. Confusing these three is one of the first mistakes beginners make.

Broker comparison

The biggest broker or the loudest broker is not automatically the right broker. The better question is: which platform gives clarity, stability, charges transparency and support for your use case?

Data-led market decisions

SARKSH GROW believes market action should come from research, structure and risk interpretation — not rumours, noise or emotional urgency.

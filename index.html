03 · Why Us

Three commitments. Audited annually.

01

Custody is yours.

Your shares sit in your demat in your name. We never hold custody of customer assets. This is enforced by regulation, and we will not deviate from it.

02

No proprietary product push.

We do not run a proprietary mutual fund, an in-house structured product, or any instrument where the fee for selling it to you is hidden inside the product itself. Our incentive is yours.

03

No lock-ins.

You can exit at any time, on your schedule. The standard regulatory exit windows for PMS apply. Beyond that, we do not impose contractual lock-ins.

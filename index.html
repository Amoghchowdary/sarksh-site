About Us

A financial institution in the making, built on clarity before action.

SARKSH GROW is a fintech company focused on scaling every person who is new to the market. The company works in the same broad market-access category as modern broking and investing platforms, but with its own operating philosophy: simpler understanding, cleaner product pathways, data-led decision support and a founder-led customer culture.

The current public stage is awareness and education. The company is preparing its broking and regulated product direction, and those services will be communicated only after applicable approvals, licenses, systems and compliance readiness.

Important public position: SARKSH GROW does not guarantee returns or outcomes. Markets involve risk. Every regulated service, advisory pathway or product feature will be offered only under applicable law and approvals. Read risk disclosure.

SARKSH PMS Portfolio pathway

Awareness
Education liveSEBI registration dependent

SARKSH PMS is the portfolio management pathway for higher-ticket portfolio conversations. PMS generally serves investors meeting regulatory eligibility norms, including the current ₹50 lakh minimum investment requirement applicable to PMS in India.

  • Suitability, documentation, risk capacity and time horizon first.
  • Clear explanation before any portfolio-level conversation.
  • Availability subject to applicable SEBI registration and product terms.

FAQ

Questions people should ask before they act.

Is SARKSH GROW a broker?

SARKSH GROW is presented as a finance and technology company focused on market education, product pathways and customer support. Public content does not claim broker status beyond what is clearly stated.

Can SARKSH tell me which stock to buy?

No public website content or free webinar should be treated as personalised advice or a stock recommendation.

Why compare brokers?

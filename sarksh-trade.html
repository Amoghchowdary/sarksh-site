Product Pathway

SARKSH Trade

A planned trading-access pathway built for CNC, MIS, margin-aware trading and long-term investment journeys through simpler UI/UX and clearer customer understanding.

Trading and broking access should not begin with a cluttered screen.

SARKSH Trade is being designed for people who want to enter the market without being overwhelmed by complicated screens, unclear order flows or noisy product language. The idea is simple: a customer should understand the platform and the risk before placing any serious trade.

CNC and long-term investing

For customers who want delivery-based investing and longer holding periods, with clearer explanation of account, order and portfolio behaviour.

MIS and intraday readiness

For customers learning intraday flow, margin awareness, risk limits, position behaviour and discipline before taking action.

What makes the product direction different

  • Simpler interface vision with less clutter and better customer understanding.
  • Clearer separation between investing, CNC, MIS, margin-aware trading and long-term investment journeys.
  • Education-first onboarding so customers know what the platform does and what it does not do.
  • Regulated broking operations will begin only after required approvals and readiness.

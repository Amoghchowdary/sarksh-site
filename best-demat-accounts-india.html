Best demat accounts: what should you compare?

Search results often show lists of the best demat accounts. Those lists are useful, but the right account depends on your own usage, support needs, charges, reporting and comfort with the platform.

Comparison framework

FactorWhat to checkWhy it matters
ChargesBrokerage, AMC, DP charges, pledge and transaction charges.Costs affect long-term usage.
PlatformMobile app, web terminal, reliability and order clarity.Confusion can cause mistakes.
SupportHuman support, escalation path and regional clarity.Problems need timely resolution.
EducationGuides, explainers and risk communication.Beginners need understanding, not only buttons.

SARKSH view

The best demat account for one person may not be the best for another. Compare the provider against your actual behaviour: investor, beginner trader, long-term holder, family account manager or active market participant.

Demat Account Creation in India | Simple Guide

Understand demat account creation in India, required documents, KYC, charges, nominee details and broker comparison basics.

What is a demat account?

A demat account holds securities in electronic form. In India, most investors need a demat account and a trading account to buy and sell listed securities through a broker or platform.

Demat account creation in India: common checklist

  • PAN card and Aadhaar-linked KYC details.
  • Bank account for fund transfer and settlement.
  • Mobile number, email and nominee details.
  • Understanding of brokerage, annual charges and DP charges.
  • Awareness of password, two-factor authentication and account safety.

Do not open blindly

Before choosing a provider, compare support, charges, platform clarity, reporting, pledge rules, statement access and customer education. The lowest charge is not always the best customer experience.
